About Us

Welcome to Joana's Café!

Founded in the heart of Bingawan, Iloilo, Joana’s Café is a locally owned coffee shop dedicated to bringing people together through the love of comforting flavors. Since opening our doors, we've taken pride in crafting specialty coffee, handmade pastries, and a whole lot of heart.

We believe a café is more than just a place to grab a drink—it's a way to connect. Whether you're relaxing with a book, chatting with friends, or working on your next big idea, our goal is to serve up not just great food and drinks, but a warm and memorable experience.

From our carefully brewed coffee to our cozy atmosphere, everything is prepared with care and love by our dedicated team. Joana’s Café isn’t just a place to eat and drink—it’s a community hub built on passion, flavor, and the joy of sharing a moment together.

Thank you for being part of our journey. We’re honored to serve the wonderful people of Bingawan and beyond.

Come curious. Leave inspired.
